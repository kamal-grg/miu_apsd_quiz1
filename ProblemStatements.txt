Contact Management System

Problem Statements (Requirement Discoery and Functional Requirement)

1.System must keep tract of contact information
2.Contact has firstname, lastname, company, jobtitle and phone(s) number, and emails(s).
3.Each phone number or email has a label such as home, mobile or work.
4.System has feature for Search contacts.
5. System must allow to add contact, update contact and delete contact.
6.system must allow to merge if duplicate found.
